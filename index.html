Coffee trivia

When did Coffee First appera in the India ?

1670 - Initial Arrival: Baba Budan planted the seeds in his hermitage, but for decades it remained a backyard garden curiosity.
18th Century - Commercialization: British entrepreneurs recognized the crop's economic potential and established the first large-scale commercial plantations in South India.
19th Century - Expansion: Commercial farming fully flourished in the mid-19th century under the British Raj. Cultivation spread from the Baba Budan Hills to other parts of the Western Ghats, including Kodagu (Coorg) in Karnataka, Wayanad in Kerala, and the Nilgiris and Shevaroys in Tamil Nadu.
